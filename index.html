Work Experience

MTech Project | Microbial and Immunotech Lab, IIT Kharagpur Jul 2023 - May 2024

Characterization of Cyclophilin Protein of Entamoeba Histolytica and Bacterial Biofilm and Entamoeba Cyst Detection Using Fluorescent Protein.

  • Optimized the expression conditions for EhCyp, GFP, JacobCBD-GFP proteins (Bacterial system).
  • Purified recombinant proteins using Ni-NTA and GSH affinity chromatography, followed by dialysis. Performed protein estimation assay (Bradford, UV spectrophotometer), SDS-PAGE and Western blotting.
  • Performed pull-down assay to study protein-protein interaction.
  • Worked with S. aureus culture and Entamoeba cell culture in the BSL-2 lab.
  • Prepared samples and analysed them using fluorescence microscope and confocal microscope.
  • Attempted to clone the calpain gene ORF but failed to obtain a positive clone.
  • Result : Developed fluorescent protein-based detection method and found no interaction between EhCyp & EhAIF.

BTech Project | Centre for Plant Molecular Biology and Biotechnology, TNAU May - Sep 2021

Objective: To gain expertise in genetic engineering techniques and bioinformatic analysis

  • Designed primer for maize pys gene using Primer3 tool and gRNA for OsDIs1 gene using CRISPR P v2.0 tool.
  • Transformation of cloned vector using Agrobacterium mediated method into a rice immature embryo.
  • Analysed transgenic plants with PCR and southern blot.
  • Gained structured insights on RNAi silencing, CRISPR Cas9 knockdown technique, MALDI-TOF, molecular docking and protein modelling.

Summer Intern | NanoBiosensors and BioDevices Lab, IIT Kharagpur May - Jul 2022

Optimization of screen mesh towards the development of SPEs for electrochemical sensing applications

  • Tested 5 substrates for screen printing and optimized the screen mesh for screen-printed electrodes (SPEs)
  • Designed the pre-treatment experiments for enhancing sensing activity of paper based SPEs
  • Gained hands-on experience in electrochemical biosensing, 3D and laser printing

Research Intern | Horticulture College and Research Institute, Periyakulam Feb - Apr 2021

Molecular characterization of different moringa genotypes and virtual screening of chemical compounds from moringa for anti-cancerous & antidiabetic properties

  • Standardized PCR condition and troubleshooted the marker analysis for 16 SSR and 24 RAPD markers.
  • Predicted drug likeliness of chemical compounds from GCMS data of Moringa extract.
  • PubChem, Protein Data Bank and PDBsum databases were used for the data collection. Open Babel, PyRx and Discovery studio were used for virtual screening.
  • Result : Predicted 5 drug-like compounds for cancer and Type 2 diabetics and found marker for PKM1 variety
